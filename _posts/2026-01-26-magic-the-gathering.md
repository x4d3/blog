**Magic: The Gathering** is a strategy card game where players cast spells, summon creatures, and use *mana* (from lands) to reduce opponents from their starting life total to **0**.

## The Basics

* You start with a hand of cards and **20 life**
  *(Commander uses **40** life)*
* You may play **one land per turn**
* Lands produce mana to cast spells
* Creatures attack **players**; opponents block with creatures
* Cards do exactly what they say â€” no hidden rules
