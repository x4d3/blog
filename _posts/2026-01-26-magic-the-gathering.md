**Magic: The Gathering** is a strategy card game where players cast spells, summon creatures, and use _mana_ (from lands) to reduce opponents from their starting life total to **0**.

## The Basics

- You start with a hand of cards and **20 life**
  _(Commander uses **40** life)_
- You may play **one land per turn**
- Lands produce mana to cast spells
- Creatures attack **players**; opponents block with creatures
- Cards do exactly what they say — no hidden rules

## The Five Colors

- **White** — order, protection
- **Blue** — control, card draw
- **Black** — sacrifice, graveyards
- **Red** — speed, direct damage
- **Green** — big creatures, ramp

## What Is Commander?

**Commander (EDH)** is a multiplayer format (usually **3–5 players**) where each player has:

- A **100-card deck**
- **1 legendary creature** as their Commander
- Only **one copy** of each card
  _(except basic lands)_
- A deck limited to the commander’s **color identity**

**Extra rule:** you lose if you take **21 combat damage from a single commander**.

Your commander starts in the **command zone** and can be cast repeatedly, costing **+2 mana** each time after the first.
